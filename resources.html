Why It Matters

Why Should I Print These?

Three good reasons, straight from the emergency department.

01

Speed Up Your Care

Knowing your medications immediately helps your care team make faster, safer decisions. Every minute matters in the ED.

02

Avoid Dangerous Errors

Drug interactions and allergy reactions are preventable when your team has complete information. Don't leave it to memory under stress.

03

Feel More in Control

The emergency department can feel overwhelming and chaotic. Having your information organized puts you back in the driver's seat of your own care.
